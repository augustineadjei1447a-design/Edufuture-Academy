Admissions Information

Start your educational journey with us. We have multiple intakes throughout the year.

Admission Requirements

For Diploma Programs

  • High School Diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 2.5/4.0
  • English proficiency test (for non-native speakers)
  • Personal statement (500 words)
  • Two letters of recommendation

For Degree Programs

  • High School Diploma with transcripts
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0/4.0
  • SAT/ACT scores (optional)
  • Personal essay (750-1000 words)
  • Three letters of recommendation
  • Interview with admissions committee
